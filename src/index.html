Nikola Tesla

This is a site dedicated to the great achievements of Nikola Tesla.

Nikola Tesla, with Rudjer Boscovich's book "Theoria Philosophiae Naturalis" in East Houston Street, New York in 1896.

  • 1856 - born in Smiljan, Autrian Empire.
  • 1861 - attended primary school im Smiljan.
  • 1870 - attended high school in Karlovac.
  • 1875 - enrolled at the Imperial-Royal Technical College in Graz.
  • 1881 - moved to Budapest to work as a chief electrican.
  • 1882 - moved to Paris to work at the Continetal Edison Company.
  • 1884 - emigrated to the US to work for Edison Machine Works in New York.
  • 1887 - developed an induction motor that run on alternating current.
  • 1889 - first experiments with his famous Tesla Coil.
  • 1896 - first work on X-rays.
  • 1898 - Tesla demonstrated the world's first radio-controlled device.
  • 1906 - developed the Tesla turbine, a bladeless centripetal flow turbine.
  • 1943 - died in New York at the age of 86.
